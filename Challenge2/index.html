Simon Says On Arduino

In this experience, I think it shows my creativity and my problem solving capabilities. During ECE 5 I was tasked to create something reflective of what was taught during the quarter and the thing I decided to recreate was the game Simon Says. I used the Arduino given to me by the school and programmed it with the materials that was taught in class. I created the circuit and code by myself and it worked after a few days of work. This took around 3 days to make with adding memory, modes, modulating speed as the user gained more and more progress, and I used the serial window to keep track of the level and whether the person lost or not. Looking back, this was a great experience because it gave me my first hands on experience in product development.
